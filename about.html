About our project

The idea for the Futility resistor sorter came from observing the waste in the 1st year Electrical and Electronic Engineering teaching laboratory, where large amounts of resistors were mixed in a large box. Most of the time, no one would use the resistors from that recycling box, which ended up being emptied and the resistors - wasted.

The Futility sorter is a unique concept designed to automatically solve this issue, without a person having to manually sort through the discarded resistors - ending up with a better use of resources and less waste produced, as well as resistor order savings.
